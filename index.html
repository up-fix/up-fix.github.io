What is Upfix?

Are you a game player? Do you enjoy watching game videos as well as playing the game? And are you also tired of watching low quality video? If yes, Upfix is for you!

Upfix is a desktop application that provides the improved video watching experience of users by converting the low quality game videos of higher quality ones.

The advantage of this application is that the users do not have to stream/watch videos at a high resolution and spend data while doing so, but instead they get to minimize their data usage and still watch the video at a higher resolution. For this purpose, Upfix takes the videos from the local storage and Upfix neural network models upscales the video by using local resources.

For now, Upfix Super Resolution Model is trained on three game types which are Chess, Among Us, and Age of Empires. The model can upscale the video by 4x.
